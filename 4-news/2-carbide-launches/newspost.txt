Title: Carbide Launches

----

Date: 2016-08-11

----

Richtext: Kevin Kwok and Guillermo Webster, both rising seniors at MIT, launched Carbide from HVF Labs today. Carbide is a new kind of programming environment that doesn’t **distinguish between input and output**, letting you run your programs backwards. It also provides new tools for visualizing and manipulating data in context via widgets—**solving one of the major problems of programming**.
